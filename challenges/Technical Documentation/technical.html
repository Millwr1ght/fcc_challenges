Introduction

At Corgis Craftery's Subsidized Arms Dealing, we read warning labels. Warning labels inform us of what horrible, horrible things might happen, if one were to use an item "wrong" enough. Take our namesake's revolutionary 'Heavy Firework Launcher' for example. When a firework is loaded with too many firework stars, it can cause severe burns and fire damage, but unfortunately, fireworks typically only fly upwards. Corgis had the brillant idea of loading a firework onto a device designed for launching heavy projectiles horizontally: the crossbow. By combining the two in a way that violates either's intended function, Corgis revolutionized the non-magical ranged combat game. We continue his legacy by reading and weaponizing warning labels.
